Jirau and Saint Antonio are a system of two dams in south eastern Amazon, Brazil. In this case study, we explore the impact of hydroelectric dams in tropical rainforest setting.
They Spey river in scotland is home to several micro-hydro projects for agriculture, aquaculture and distilleries.
The Syr Darya river in Kazakhstan is bounded by two dams. Chardara, upstream on the border with Uzbekistan, provides electricity and vital irrigation for the arid surrounding land. Kok Aral, downstream on the drying Aral Sea, prevents water flowing into the Uzbek South Aral sea.
Tehri dam was constructed in 2005 in Uttarakhand, India. The dam provides electricity and freshwater, but has had many impacts on the populations close to the dam.
